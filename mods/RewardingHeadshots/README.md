# Rewarding Headshots and Damage

This mod increases the damage dealth to human NPCs and drastically increases the damage that headshots do on human NPCs.

Bosses and special squads are less affected by this mod.

# Details

## 8 January 2026 v2

1. Increases damage dealt to head, body, and limbs by 45%, 20%, and 10% respectively.
2. Zombies' damage dealt increased by 30%, 10%, and reduced by 10% respectively.
3. Bosses and special squads damage either set to 3x, 2x, 0.5x or increased by 150%, 75%, and reduced by 50% respectively.
4. Affects ** ONLY ** Player -> Human NPC combat

## First Version v1

1. Increases damage dealt to head, body, and limbs by 50%, 30%, and 20% respectively.
2. Zombies' damage dealt increased by 30%, 30%, 25% respectively.
3. Bosses and special squads damage either set to 4, 2, 0.5 or increased by 200%, 100%, and 20% respectively.
4. Affects Player -> Human NPC combat
